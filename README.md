# Environmental Time-Scribe 🌍

An interactive solar panel illustration that visualizes the day cycle with dynamic sky colors and environmental animations.

## 🌟 Features

- **Interactive Time Slider**: Scrub through a complete 24-hour day cycle
- **Dynamic Sky Colors**: Watch the sky transform from midnight blues through dawn pastels, noon blues, and sunset oranges
- **Auto-Cycle Playback**: Automatically cycle through the day with the auto-play button
- **Responsive Design**: Beautiful animations and visual effects that adapt to your screen
- **Environmental Illustration**: Interactive graphics showing solar panels and environmental elements throughout the day

## 🎮 How to Use

1. Open the [live demo](https://solar-panel-orcin.vercel.app/) in your browser
2. Use the slider to control the time of day (0 = Midnight, 100 = Midnight next day)
3. Click the **Auto-Cycle** button to automatically progress through a complete day cycle
4. Watch the sky colors and animations change in real-time

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
